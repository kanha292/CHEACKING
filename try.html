About the Event
The Cisco India & SAARC Manufacturing Summit 2025 is a premier platform to unlock the future of manufacturing where innovation meets action. This one-day, power-packed event brings together CXOs, plant heads, and digital transformation leaders to explore strategies that drive resilience, agility, and sustainability.

From bridging IT and OT silos to securing industrial environments, enhancing production uptime, and leveraging AI-driven insights, the summit delivers actionable takeaways through live demos, real-world case studies, and collaborative discussions. Participants will gain early access to Cisco’s manufacturing roadmap and connect with peers and industry experts, all focused on enabling smarter, faster, and future-ready manufacturing.
